Frequently Asked Questions

Who can see my submitted questions?

Only administrators can see your submitted questions, other students can not.

How long does it usually take to get a reply?

You can expect a response within 3-5 working days, however depending on the volume of questions, this may be longer or shorter.

I forgot my password. How do I recover it?

Click the forgot password button and follow the link in your email to reset your password

How do I report a problem that I found in your application?

Email an administrator
